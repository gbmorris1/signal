Privacy Policy

ODDIQ · Last updated: 17 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ODDIQ, a product of Gains Academy LLC (“ODDIQ,” “we,” “us”), collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ODDIQ mobile application (the “App”). ODDIQ is a research and analysis tool for publicly available prediction markets. By using the App you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

AccountEmail address, and preferences you choose (categories of interest, experience level)To create and secure your account and personalize your feed

We do not collect your name, phone number, mailing address, precise (GPS) location, contacts, photos, or social-media logins. We do not knowingly sell your personal information.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the App and your account.
  • To personalize your market briefing and AI analysis.
  • To process subscriptions and enforce plan limits.
  • To send transactional messages (email confirmation, password reset) and, if enabled, market alerts.
  • To analyze usage and improve the product.
  • To comply with law and enforce our Terms.

3. Service providers (sub-processors)

We share the minimum data necessary with providers who process it on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and hosting.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement tracking.
  • OpenRouter / Anthropic and Tavily — AI analysis generation and news retrieval (they receive the market being analyzed, not your identity).
  • Expo — push-notification delivery.
  • Apple — payment processing for subscriptions (we never receive your card details).

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or UK, we process your data under: contract (to provide the App and your subscription), legitimate interests (to secure and improve the App), consent (for optional push notifications), and legal obligation. You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, and to object to processing or withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us below. You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

5. Your California rights (CCPA/CPRA & CalOPPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact us below.

6. Data retention

We keep account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Profile → Delete account, or by contacting us), we permanently delete your account and associated data. Aggregated or de-identified data that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encryption in transit, row-level access controls, and provider-managed infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The App is intended for users 17 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International transfers

We and our providers may process your information in the United States and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date, and where required we will provide additional notice.
